The gardener

i chose you
and promised you forever.
the first time we met you looked like a dying hopeless plant
and i promised to be the gardener of your heart
removing all the weeds that suffocated you
and patch the holes that he left behind. 
i took good care of your heart
watered it with love
and made sure i was always the source of all its nourishment
it almost took forever for you to get back on our feet
but again i had all the time to keep my promise
as i took care of it i began fading away,
with breath slowly leaving my love life
and before i knew it i was dead in the inside 
as i watered you with all of me
"only love can heal what love has broken"
so they say
