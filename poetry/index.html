...... Gem ......

guard your beauty like someone is trying to break in and steal it, for a woman life is a battle, and beauty is their machine gun, if they can see where your makeup ends and your face begin, you have done it wrong, makeup is a face not a mask you have to blend, indifference is the best revenge, beauty that matters is the one from the inside, woman ill prepared is set up for failure, if you let tour gut down everyone can get in. They say beauty lies on the eyes of the beholder.

..... JOE ......

I write to you dear friend, I know it hurts to see everthing drowning before your eyes, but at the end of the day, you will get past it as you have always done. your silent tears are speaking louder, I might not really understand the pain, but i know the feeling.
